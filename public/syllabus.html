Audience

Professionals new to AI who need practical workplace literacy — not a coding bootcamp.

After V1, learners can explain core AI concepts, use tools responsibly, write better prompts, and apply AI to everyday work tasks with critical judgment.

Assessment

Each module ends with a 5-question quiz (pass 4/5). The final exam has 25 questions (pass 18/25). A personalized certificate is available after exam pass.

How to take the course

Open the live site, enter your name, complete modules in any order (exam unlocks after all modules), then download your certificate. Progress is saved in your browser.
